Help Ukraine

This website lists authentic sources where people can help Ukraine and its citizen during the war with Russia, ongoing since 24 February 2021.

How you can help

Attend manifestations nearby

Search for "ukraine" on Facebook Events:
https://www.facebook.com/events/search/?q=ukraine

Donate money

The National Bank of Ukraine has opened a fundraising account to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine:
https://bank.gov.ua/en/news/all/natsionalniy-bank-vidkriv-spetsrahunok-dlya-zboru-koshtiv-na-potrebi-armiyi

Bank account numbers are on the website. You can Google the IBAN code between double quotes to verify it (e.g. "DE05504000005040040066")

Offer housing & other help for refugees

MAPAHELP lists all available help services for Ukrainian refugees in Europe and abroad.

Offer your services here:


https://mapahelp.me/

Contact local Ukrainian embassies

Many Ukrainian embassies in Europe and abroad are open to receive help. You can call, email or get in touch via their social media channels.

Is this website reliable?

I hope so. I have several family and relatives assisting me with information. My goal is to offer accurate, real information in one place. I try to always link to the websites, so you can verify sources yourself. Never believe anything you read without verification. Never send any money to any bank accounts if you are uncertain of their owners. Always do your own research!
